How to play

Find all of the pieces on a board that are similar to the special "Me" piece. Pieces change after each board is cleared.

Step 1

Notice me: my shape, color, and pattern.

Step 2

Select all pieces that share at least two of my attributes (color and shape, shape and pattern, or pattern and color).

Step 3

Click me to see if you got them all!
